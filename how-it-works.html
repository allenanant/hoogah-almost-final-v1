The Process

How Hoogah Works

Three simple steps turn a room full of strangers into a network of meaningful connections. No app downloads. No friction. Just intentional design that makes every attendee feel like they belong.

Step by Step

From Arrival to Authentic Connection

Hoogah integrates into your event workflow in minutes. Here is exactly what happens from the moment your attendees walk through the door.

01

Attendees Answer Questions

When attendees arrive, they scan a QR code displayed at your venue. In under 90 seconds they answer 4-5 thoughtful questions that you have crafted through your admin dashboard. These are not generic icebreakers. They are tailored to your event's theme, industry, and goals. Questions might explore what attendees hope to learn, the challenges they are facing, or the ideas they are most excited about. The answers paint a rich picture of who each person is and what they need from the room.

Fully Customizable
02

Hoogah Creates Intentional Pairings

Our algorithm reads between the lines. It does not just match keywords. It identifies complementary perspectives, shared values, and the kind of overlapping curiosity that leads to real dialogue. Pairings can be 1-on-1 for deep connection, or huddles of 3-6 people for collaborative energy. The system accounts for diverse backgrounds, avoids repeat pairings across rounds, and ensures every single person in the room is matched, including the quiet ones who might otherwise be left standing alone.

Intentional, Not Random
03

Real Conversations Start

Each pair receives a personalized conversation starter drawn directly from their responses, along with a designated meeting spot at your venue. No more awkward introductions. No more "so, what do you do?" From minute one, attendees step into conversations that feel relevant, human, and worth having. The result is that people leave your event remembering who they talked to, not just what they heard from the stage.

Under the Hood

The Engine Behind
Every Pairing

Hoogah is not a random name generator. Our matching engine processes attendee responses through multiple layers of analysis to find the pairings that will spark the most meaningful conversations.

It evaluates semantic similarity, complementary expertise, shared goals, and diversity of perspective. The result is that every pairing feels intentional because it is.

For multi-round events, the system tracks previous pairings so attendees never get matched with the same person twice. Each round surfaces new connections, new ideas, and new reasons to come back.

hoogah_engine.py
# Hoogah Pairing Engine
def create_pairings(attendees):
  responses = parse_answers(attendees)
  vectors = embed_interests(responses)
  graph = build_affinity_matrix(vectors)
  pairs = optimize_matching(graph)
  # Generate icebreakers
  for pair in pairs:
    pair.starter = craft_icebreaker(
      shared = pair.common_threads,
      contrast = pair.unique_angles,
      tone = "warm_and_curious"
    )
  return pairs

FAQ

Common Questions, Straight Answers

Everything you need to know before you bring Hoogah to your next event.

Do my attendees need to download an app?

No. Hoogah is entirely browser-based. Attendees scan a QR code with their phone camera and answer the questions right in their mobile browser. No downloads, no logins, no friction.

How long does the questionnaire take?

Most attendees complete it in 60 to 90 seconds. The questions are short, engaging, and designed to feel more like a conversation than a form. We have seen completion rates above 95%.

Can I run multiple pairing rounds at one event?

Absolutely. Many organizers run 2 to 3 rounds during an event. The algorithm ensures no one gets paired with the same person twice and each round introduces fresh connections.

What size events does Hoogah work for?

Hoogah works for intimate gatherings of 20 people all the way up to conferences with 500+ attendees. The matching engine scales automatically and maintains quality at any size.

How do attendees find their match?

After pairing, each attendee receives a notification on their phone with their match's name, a personalized conversation starter, and a suggested meeting spot at your venue. Simple and seamless.

How long does it take to set up Hoogah for my event?

Most organizers are fully set up in under 10 minutes. You create your event, customize your questions, and generate a QR code. Our onboarding team is available to help if you need it.
