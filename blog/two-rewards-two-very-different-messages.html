Two Rewards. Two Very Different Messages.

Khushi Yadav & Joe Lovett March 12, 2026

Most companies reward performance.

But not all of them acknowledge the person behind it.

Bonuses reward performance.
Vacation acknowledges the person.

Both matter.

But they send very different signals about what organizations truly value.

One says:
“We appreciate what you produced.”

The other says:
“We value you beyond your output.”

Great workplaces usually understand the difference.

Which signal do you think companies emphasize more today?
