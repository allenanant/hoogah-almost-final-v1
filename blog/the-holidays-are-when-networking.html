The Holidays Are When Networking Becomes Human Again

A reflection on belonging, intention, and why small talk falls short

Joe Lovett & Khushi Yadav December 17, 2025

But first… Hoogah In Action
Where we’re currently building connection

Events: 9
Communities: 4
Workshops and Training: 1
Employers: 1

During the holidays, connection feels easy.
You spend time with people who know your history, your values, your quirks.
Shared memories replace introductions.
You skip small talk because shared context already exists.

At work and at events, we often do the opposite.
We optimize for volume, not meaning.
We mistake proximity for connection and hope something sticks.

But real connection does not happen by accident.
It happens with intention.

When people are paired with purpose.
When conversations move past titles and transactions.
When they have direction and context.
People leave feeling understood, not just exchanged.

That is the feeling the holidays bring back.
Not more interaction.
Better, deeper connection.

At Hoogah, we try to bring that same feeling into workplaces and events.
This belief guides everything we build.
Not by adding more interaction,
but by making connection easier, more human, and more intentional.

Because culture is not created in crowded rooms.
It is built one conversation at a time.

Happy Holidays from all of us at Hoogah
