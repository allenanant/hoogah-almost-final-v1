The Flow

Before, during, after.

The same simple loop fits inside every event format.

1 Before

You choose the questions

Create a few short questions based on what matters for your event. Theme, career stage, future goals, current challenges, interests.

2 During

Attendees are matched

Based on their answers, attendees are matched with others who share similar interests, goals, or mindsets.

3 Conversation

Better conversations, faster

They already know who they're meeting and why. So they move past small talk into something more meaningful.

4 After

You see what happened

Who connected, what mattered, how engaged people were. You're not guessing whether networking worked.
