FAQ

Everything you might want to know.

16 quick answers about pricing, setup, format, participation, and what happens during your event.

1. How does pricing work?

+

You're charged $20 per attendee who participates in Hoogah. Most organizers include this in their ticket price. You only pay for people who actually use it.

2. How long does setup take?

+

About 45 minutes the first time. For future events, closer to 15 minutes. You create your event, add a few questions, and you're ready. We'll guide you through it.

3. Will this disrupt the flow of my event?

+

No. Hoogah fits within the networking structure you already have, like breaks, receptions, or dedicated networking time.

4. Do attendees need to download anything?

+

Yes. Attendees access Hoogah by scanning a QR code and downloading the app. For larger events, Hoogah can also be integrated into your existing event app.

5. Will this feel forced or awkward?

+

No. No one is forced to participate. Hoogah simply gives attendees a clear way into conversations.

6. What if attendees don't participate?

+

Participation is typically high because people know who to talk to and why. Even partial participation improves the experience. You can also see how many attendees connected and who they met.

7. What size events does this work for?

+

Hoogah works well for groups of 20 to 2,000+. The experience scales based on your event size.

8. Can I customize how people are matched?

+

Yes. You choose the questions attendees answer, based on what matters for your event. This could include goals, roles, interests, or themes.

9. When does this happen during the event?

+

Usually during networking moments. Some organizers start as early as a Day 0 reception so attendees arrive on Day 1 already feeling connected.

10. What kind of events is this best for?

+

Any event where connection matters. That includes conferences, executive summits, sales kickoffs, internal events, and training programs.

11. What do I get after the event?

+

You'll see who connected, what attendees cared about, and how engagement played out. Attendees can also rate conversations from 1 to 5, giving you another layer of feedback beyond surveys.

12. Do I need to change my agenda?

+

No. Hoogah works within the networking structure you already have.

13. What if my event is canceled?

+

You owe nothing.

14. What happens during the event itself?

+

Hoogah is designed to support the full experience. Some events include dedicated Hoogah networking areas with staffed support and a physical setup that makes it easy for attendees to meet.

15. Why not just let networking happen naturally?

+

Because it usually doesn't. Every other part of your event is carefully designed. Networking is often left to chance. Hoogah makes sure that part actually works.

16. What makes this different from typical networking?

+

Most networking relies on chance. Hoogah is designed. Attendees are guided into conversations with context, so something more meaningful can happen.
